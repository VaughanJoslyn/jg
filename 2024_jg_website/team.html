Our Team

Greg Joslyn

Managing Director

Greg Joslyn is Joslyn Group's founder and managing director. Greg works in close partnership with all clients to achieve outstanding outcomes.

Greg personally manages every aspect of your project, right from the beginning, to completion and beyond.

With 25 years' experience in project managing, conceptualising and delivering excellent turf solutions, he is known as one of Australia's leading sports builders, and has a long history and experience in earth moving and civil construction.

Greg's skills and experience was honed whilst working as part of the family business, which was a role he held for decades before venturing out on his own, by starting Joslyn Group in 2014.

Greg's outstanding commitment to excellence combined with a wealth of knowledge and practical experience, ensures that Joslyn Group produces a superior, international-standard surface for every sports ground, every time.

Paul Christian

Director

Paul Christian has a wealth of experience, having run his family business started by his dad, growing it to 12 people. During this time, he partnered directly with Joslyn Group, until finally joining forces with them, becoming the company's director. Paul's expertise and skill ensures each project undertaken is extremely well-managed and completed to the highest quality.

Paul brings more than 25 years' experience in civil construction, having specialised in roadworks, carparks, landscaping, wetlands, subdivisions and more. He is the ultimate pro when it comes to understanding and dealing with the intricacies and challenges of a site. It's one of the many reasons our clients regard him so highly - they have every confidence to trust that Paul will deliver on his promises.

Steve Crankshaw

Site Supervisor

Steve brings to the team more than 20 years' experience in the construction industry. In his role as a site supervisor, he ensures that the wheels keep turning on site, and that the day-to-day operations run efficiently and safely. He is an effective communicator with a partuicular interest in training and learning. His passion for achieving high quality outcomes has lead to him playing a key role in our projects including Seabrook Reserve.

Peter Keane

Site Supervisor

Keane is known in the industry as being a motivated and hardworking site supervisor. He is responsible for the day-to-day management with the construction team, and ensures the timely delivery of projects. He brings more than 12 years' experience, and an in-depth knowledge of the sports field construction history. His focus on building long-lasting bonds, creating strong working relationships with his team, clients, stakeholders and subcontractors places him as invaluable in the industry.
