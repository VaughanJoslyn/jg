About us

Welcome to Joslyn Group, an independently run business with deep experience in natural and synthetic turf and civil construction. You can rely on us to deliver on time and on budget.

As a family-owned business with deep experience in earth-moving engineering and natural and synthetic turf, we get great satisfaction from producing beautiful, professionally certified playing fields and surfaces for people to enjoy.

You can rely on us to deliver on time and on budget – we guarantee the longevity of our workmanship because we want the job done right. We own all our own equipment, which means we can offer value for money. Our team has more than 100 years' collective experience in providing quality solutions across a range of environments and disciplines, so we definitely know what it takes to get the specific details right.


A History in Turf

Joslyn is a well-known name in the turf business.

In February 2014, Greg and Cassie Joslyn founded Joslyn Group. They saw a need for a high-quality personalised service that was built on trust and integrity. In 2020, they joined forces with Diamond Valley Excavations, which expanded their team to 30+ staff with a variety of experience in all facets of natural and synthetic, earthmoving engineering and excavation services.

Their clients and projects include the City of Whittlesea, Monash City Council, the home of the Matildas - La Trobe University, Epping Recreation Reserve, Aegis Park - the home of the North Melbourne Football Club and others.
